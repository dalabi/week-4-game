Crystals Collector: Poketto Monsuta Edition!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystal themed games below. By clicking on a game, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each game is hidden from you until you click it.
